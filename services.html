FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services

What is your minimum order quantity?

Our minimum order quantity varies by product. For spices, it's typically 500 kg, while for vegetables it's 1000 kg. However, we're flexible for first-time orders. Contact us to discuss your specific requirements.

Which countries do you export to?

We export to 50+ countries worldwide including USA, UK, UAE, Saudi Arabia, Singapore, Malaysia, Australia, Canada, and most European and Middle Eastern nations. If your country isn't listed, please contact us.

How long does delivery take?

Delivery time depends on destination and shipping method. Air freight takes 3-7 days while sea freight takes 15-30 days. We provide exact delivery timelines with every quote and guarantee on-time delivery.

Do you provide product samples?

Yes! We highly recommend ordering samples before bulk orders. We can send samples (shipping charges may apply) so you can verify quality firsthand. Once satisfied, we'll proceed with your bulk order.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept bank wire transfers (SWIFT/NEFT/RTGS), Letter of Credit (LC), and for established clients, we offer flexible payment terms. All transactions are secure and documented per international trade regulations.

Are your products certified organic?

Yes, we offer both conventional and certified organic products. Our organic range is certified by USDA Organic, EU Organic, and India Organic standards. All products come with proper certification documents.
